# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

*Last updated: May 2026*

## English

### 1. Information Collection
All data (item information, family members, barcode cache, app settings) is stored **only locally** on your device's SQLite database. No data is automatically uploaded to any server.

### 2. Camera Permission
Barcode scanning requires camera permission. The camera is used exclusively for scanning product barcodes. No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted.

### 3. Network Requests
When you use the barcode lookup feature, we send the barcode number to third-party APIs (Open Food Facts) to retrieve product information. These services may log your request for API operation purposes.

### 4. Nearby Devices Permission
Family sync uses Google's Nearby Connections API over WiFi/Bluetooth to discover nearby devices and transfer item data. This requires location or nearby devices permission, used only when you actively enable this feature.

### 5. File Storage
Exporting backups and importing photos requires storage read/write permission. Exported files contain only item information and barcode data, no personal identity information.

### 6. In-App Purchases
When you make a purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play Store. The developer does not collect or store any payment information.

### 7. Third-Party Services
This app uses several third-party SDKs (sqflite, mobile_scanner, flutter_local_notifications, etc.) which may collect device information to provide their services. See the SDK list inside the app for details.

### 8. Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact: **lovesmile811@gmail.com**

## 中文

### 1. 信息收集
本应用所有数据（物品信息、家庭成员、条码缓存、应用设置）**仅存储于您设备的本地 SQLite 数据库中**，不会自动上传至任何服务器。

### 2. 相机权限
条码扫描功能需要相机权限，仅用于扫描物品条码。不会拍摄、存储或传输任何图像或视频。

### 3. 网络请求
使用条码查询功能时，我们会向第三方 API 服务（Open Food Facts）发送条码编号以获取物品信息。这些服务可能记录您的请求以提供 API 服务。

### 4. 附近设备权限
家庭同步功能使用 Google Nearby Connections API，通过 WiFi/蓝牙在局域网内发现附近设备并传输物品数据。此功能需要位置或附近设备权限，仅在您主动开启时使用。

### 5. 文件存储
导出备份和导入照片时需要读写存储权限。导出的文件仅包含物品信息和条码数据，不含个人身份信息。

### 6. 应用内购买
当您进行购买时，交易完全由 Google Play Store 处理。开发者不会收集或存储任何支付信息。

### 7. 第三方服务
本应用使用了若干第三方 SDK（sqflite、mobile_scanner、flutter_local_notifications 等），这些 SDK 可能收集设备信息以提供服务。详情请查看应用内的第三方 SDK 清单。

### 8. 联系我们
如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系：**lovesmile811@gmail.com**
